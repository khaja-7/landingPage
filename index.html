About Ekalavya

Welcome to the Ekalavya Platform! This comprehensive educational ecosystem is designed to provide secure and user-friendly access for three main groups:

Students (Up to 12th)

Access personalized study materials, track progress, and connect with mentors for academic support.

UG Students

Manage coursework, collaborate on projects, and participate in academic communities to enhance your learning experience.

Institutions

Oversee student activities, manage resources, and analyze educational outcomes to improve institutional effectiveness.

This project aims to simplify and secure the educational process for different user groups. With a modern UI and robust backend, Ekalavya ensures a seamless experience for students and institutions alike.

Key Features

Mentorship

Connect with experienced mentors who provide personalized guidance and support throughout your educational journey.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your academic progress with detailed analytics and insights to identify areas for improvement.

Personalized Study Plans

Get customized study plans tailored to your learning style, goals, and academic requirements.

Collaboration Tools

Work together with peers through integrated collaboration features and group study sessions.
